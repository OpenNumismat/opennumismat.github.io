Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28.03.2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application OpenNumismat for Windows devices.

1. No Data Collection

OpenNumismat does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, usage data, or private files on external servers. All data you enter into the application (collections, photos, prices, personal notes) is stored locally on your device in a database file chosen by you.

2. Permissions and Their Use

The application may request the following permissions only to provide its core features:

Internet Access: Required only if the user manually initiates a request to download exchange rates, import data from online catalogs (e.g., Colnect), or check for software updates. No personal data is sent during these requests.

File System: Required to save and load your collection databases and images on your local drive.

3. Third-Party Services

OpenNumismat may provide links or integration with third-party numismatic services. We do not share your personal data with these services. Interaction with these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated text within the application or its official documentation.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the application, please contact us via the official website or repository.
