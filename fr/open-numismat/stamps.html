OpenNumismat for stamp collection

Description

OpenNumismat was developed for coin collectors, but it also customizable for other collectibles, like stamps, banknotes, postcards, badges.

With OpenNumismat, you will be able to organize and manage stamp collection: create an advanced catalogue, wish and selling lists, view statistics for your collection.
